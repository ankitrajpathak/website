About me

The person
behind the campaigns.

I'm Ankit — a B2B marketer who believes the best marketing doesn't ask for attention, it earns it. Most brands spend fortunes sounding like everyone else. I prefer the opposite.

Currently the sole marketer at Zeron, a venture-backed cybersecurity startup in Mumbai — which means I've owned everything: brand, campaigns, SEO, paid media, events, PR, and product launches. Not as a generalist who dabbles, but as someone who had to make each thing actually work.

Before Zeron, I ran performance campaigns at Vehere and orchestrated large-scale political operations at I-PAC. My work spans industries, but the principle stays the same: clarity first, then craft.
